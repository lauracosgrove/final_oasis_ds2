Risk allele e4, intra_cranial_vol, lh_cortex_vol, rh_cortical_white_matter_vol, and sub_cort_gray_vol are all significant. Unfortunately, sub_cort_gray_vol is not in the expected direction, and intracranial volume really shouldn’t carry any information. We saw some funny results with sub_cort_gray_vol in EDA; perhaps we should go with total gray volume as a coefficient instead.
